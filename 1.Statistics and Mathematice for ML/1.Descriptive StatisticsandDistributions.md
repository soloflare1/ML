### **Z-score (Standard Score)**

**What it is:**

* Tells **how far a number is from the mean** in terms of **standard deviations**.
* Basically, it **normalizes data** so we can compare different features easily.

**Formula (super simple):**
[
Z = \frac{X - \text{mean}}{\text{SD}}
]

Where:

* (X) = your number
* mean = average of data
* SD = standard deviation


---

**Why in ML:**

1. Helps **normalize data** (so features with big numbers donâ€™t dominate).
